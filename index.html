He is passionate about using AI and data-driven approaches to optimize results for better decision-making. Early Life and Education

Amey was born and raised in Jammu & Kashmir, India. He is currently pursuing a B.S. in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, and a minor in Mathematics.
